## When to Use For Loops vs. While Loops

**For loops** are typically best for fixed-size traversals—when you know exactly how many times to loop.

**While loops** shine when:

- You don't know in advance how many steps you'll take
- You're waiting for a certain condition to become true

**Examples where while loops are a good fit:**

- Traversing a linked list until you reach the end
- Reading user input until they type "exit"
- Searching for a value in a structure where you don't know the length
